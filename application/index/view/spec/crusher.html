FIVE ADVANTAGES SHOW THE PERFECT DESIGN

  • 1.Highly efficient and flexible processing capacity

    Optimized modes of grates pre-screening on vibrating feeder, adjustable installation angle on vibrating screen and speed-adjustable mode on trailer belts maximize the processing capacity in crushing sand and gravel.

  • 2.Optimal emission reduction and noise reduction

    The design of water-spray dustproof device, rubber skin in the position of discharging opening and material receiving are added to effectively reduce the noise and dust emissions, so as to minimize the impact on the environment.

  • 3.Most humanized structure design

    The humanity design, which includes the maintenance of lifting frame, hooks and operation system, tools of maintenance, toolbox and centralized lubrication, the electrical signal over-loaded alarm system and hydraulic controlling system, reduces the risk to a minimum.

  • 4.The latest modular design

    The material for the production are upgraded from Q235A steel to Q345B steel with higher strength, combined with interchangeable system of main machines and the versatility of one trailer, so it is suitable to deal with changeful stone material processing requirements in remote areas.

  • 5.Most flexible solution configuration

    According to different crushing process requirements can be composed of different models, with high flexibility to meet the various needs of customers.
