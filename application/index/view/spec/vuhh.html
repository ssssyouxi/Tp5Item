Opportunities

1、Infrastructure construction tide keeps surging and the demand for aggregate is brisk.

2、The price of sand and stone is in a state of rising and fluctuating, and the prospect is broad.

3、There is a large supply gap in some areas, so the sand aggregate faces a huge investment opportunity.

Challenges

1、The decline of natural sand reserves led to the government's policy of limiting mining, which could not make aggregate supplied continuously.

2、The quality of mixture consisting of nature sand and concrete is decreased because the quality of nature sand is decreased, so that the construction quality is also influenced.

3、The regional distribution is uneven, and the cost of raw material collection is high.

4、Traditional sand making uses cheap jaw crusher to produce. It takes disadvantages of simple crushing, single screening and unstable quality.

Based on Japan's special design and advanced international technology, Kefid developed an integrated sand making system --VU aggregate optimization system. With the integration technology of breaking, grinding and sand selecting, VU aggregate optimization system can produce machine-made sand comparable to natural sand, which can well meet the demand of construction market.
