INDUSTRY BACKGROUND

The rapid development of global infrastructure and environmental problems become increasingly serious, in the natural sand resources are depleted and the Government of natural sand mining control efforts to increase the situation, the sand industry ushered in a new development opportunities, but also on the sand production system put forward higher requirements.

The high-quality sand production line is composed of vibration feeder, jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, vibrating screen, belt conveyor and intelligent electronic control. According to the customer of different processing materials and finished product size of the needs of the combination and classification, the output of finished sand, due to grade continuous, granular rounded, with powder control and other advantages by the majority of customers of all ages.
