With the increase of coal consumption and the increasing pressure of environmental pollution caused by sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides, desulfurization for dust removal is imminent. At present, the limestone flue gas desulfurization (FGD) method has become the most mature and reliable FGD technology, which is widely used in the field of thermal power plant, iron and steel industry, and the desulfurization efficiency can be as high as 90%. In recent years, with the continuous increase of environmental protection, we have higher requirements for granularity of limestone powder. Therefore, finding a low cost, high fineness of limestone powder preparation equipment has become customer's primary goal. As the special equipment for preparing of desulphurizer, the Vertical grinding mill (VM) and MTW European grinding mill launched by Kefid are very popular in the field of desulphurization in power plants, which are also widely used in the field of limestone powder making.

Technological process

STAGE
1

Raw Materials Crushing
The limestone bulk material is broken by the crusher to the feeding fineness (15mm-50mm) that can enter the mill.

STAGE
2

Grinding
After crushing, the small pieces of limestone will be sent to the hopper by the hoist, and then evenly and quantitatively delivered to the mill chamber by the feeder.

STAGE
3

Grading
After grinding, the material will be graded by the grading system. The unqualified powder will be sent back to the main engine for regrinding.

STAGE
4

Collecting Product
The qualified powder will be separated and collected through the pipe into the dust collector, the collected powder will be sent to the finished product bin through the conveying device through the discharge port, and then packed in a powder packed tank car or an automatic packer.
