Construction waste is a solid waste produced in the process of building, reconstructing, expanding or demolishing a building. With the accelerated process of urbanization, construction waste has attracted more and more attention from the government as an environmental problem. If construction waste is not properly handled, it will bring a lot of bad effects. The random stowage of construction waste will cause potential safety hazards and will affect urban traffic. Construction waste will seriously pollute water resources and affect air quality. Besides, it will occupy land and reduce soil quality.
